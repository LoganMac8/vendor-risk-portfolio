Vendor Security Questionnaire (25 Questions)
Third-Party Risk & Security Assessment

1. Access Control
1. Do you enforce multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all administrative and remote access accounts?
2. Do you follow a least-privilege access model when granting access to users?
3. Are access rights reviewed on a regular basis (e.g., quarterly)?
4. Are user accounts for terminated or transferred employees removed or modified promptly?

2. Policies, Governance & HR Security
5. Do you maintain formal, documented security policies that are reviewed at least annually?
6. Are all employees required to sign confidentiality or non-disclosure agreements?
7. Do employees receive recurring security awareness training (e.g., phishing, data handling)?
8. Do you perform background checks on employees, where legally permitted?

3. Asset Management & Data Protection
9. Do you classify data according to sensitivity (e.g., public, internal, confidential)?
10. Is data encrypted in transit using modern protocols (e.g., TLS 1.2 or higher)?
11. Is data encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption methods?
12. Do you have controls to detect or prevent unauthorized data exfiltration?

4. Logging, Monitoring & Incident Response
13. Do you maintain centralized logging and monitoring for security-relevant events?
14. Do you have a formal incident response plan that outlines roles, responsibilities, and escalation steps?
15. Has your incident response plan been tested within the past 12 months?
16. Will you notify customers promptly in the event of a security incident or data breach?

5. Cloud Security & Shared Responsibility
17. Do you use a major cloud service provider (e.g., AWS, Azure, GCP), and do you adhere to the shared responsibility model?
18. Are your cloud environments configured with segmentation, least privilege, and encryption?
19. Do you regularly assess cloud security configurations (e.g., automated scans, CIS benchmark reviews)?

6. Subservice Providers / Supply Chain Risk
20. Do you use third-party subprocessors or subservice providers to deliver your service?
21. Do you assess the security posture of your subprocessors (e.g., SOC 2 reviews, ISO certificates, questionnaires)?
22. Do you maintain an up-to-date list of subprocessors and notify customers of any changes?

7. Change Management & Secure Development
23. Do you follow a formal change management process that includes review and approval steps?
24. Do you use secure development practices such as code review, vulnerability scanning, and security testing?
25. Do you conduct regular penetration tests or independent security assessments of your platform?
