Vendor Security Questionnaire

A streamlined security questionnaire designed to evaluate a vendor’s ability to protect data, maintain compliance, and operate securely. This questionnaire combines elements from SOC 2 Trust Service Criteria, ISO 27001 Annex A controls, SIG Lite, and the CAIQ framework.

Section One Organizational Security

One Does the vendor maintain formal information security policies that are reviewed and approved at least annually
Two Does the vendor have a defined security governance structure with assigned responsibilities
Three Does the vendor conduct background screening for employees prior to granting access to systems or data
Four Does the vendor require recurring security awareness training for all personnel

Section Two Access Control

Five Does the vendor enforce least privilege access for all users
Six Does the vendor use multi factor authentication for administrative or sensitive access
Seven Does the vendor review user access rights on a recurring basis
Eight Does the vendor have a process for removing access promptly when employees change roles or leave the organization

Section Three Data Protection

Nine Does the vendor encrypt sensitive data during storage and transmission
Ten Does the vendor classify data based on sensitivity and apply appropriate safeguards
Eleven Does the vendor maintain secure data retention and deletion practices
Twelve Does the vendor prevent unauthorized copying or transfer of data

Section Four Infrastructure and Operations

Thirteen Does the vendor perform vulnerability scanning and timely patching
Fourteen Does the vendor perform regular penetration tests or independent security assessments
Fifteen Does the vendor monitor systems and networks for suspicious activity
Sixteen Does the vendor maintain documented change management procedures
Seventeen Does the vendor maintain tested backups and recovery procedures

Section Five Application and Product Security

Eighteen Are applications developed following secure coding practices
Nineteen Does the vendor test applications for security issues before release
Twenty Does the vendor have processes to address reported vulnerabilities or responsible disclosures

Section Six Incident Response

Twenty One Does the vendor maintain an incident response plan
Twenty Two Does the vendor notify customers of incidents within a defined timeframe
Twenty Three Has the vendor experienced a security incident in the past twenty four months and if so is a summary available

Section Seven Compliance and Certifications

Twenty Four Does the vendor maintain relevant certifications or assessments such as SOC Two or ISO Twenty Seven Thousand One
Twenty Five Does the vendor comply with privacy regulations that apply to the data handled such as GDPR CCPA or HIPAA

Summary

This questionnaire provides a high level yet comprehensive security evaluation suitable for vendor onboarding, reassessment, and ongoing monitoring. Answers should be reviewed alongside SOC Two reports, ISO certifications, penetration test summaries, and risk scoring results to form a complete picture of the vendor’s security posture.
